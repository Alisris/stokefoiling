FAQ

Is the Stoke Foil Boost suitable for beginners?

Yes, the Stoke Foil Boost is designed for foilers of all skill levels, including beginners. The stable design and user-friendly components make it ideal for those new to foiling.

Is the Stoke Foil Boost suitable for experienced foilers?

Definitely! The Stoke Foil Boost makes foiling possible on light wind days, with no drag when the foil is flying. If you want more time out foiling, then the Stoke Foil Boost is for you.

What maintenance is required for the Stoke Foil Boost?

Maintenance is minimal with the Stoke Foil Boost. Regularly inspecting the unit for any signs of wear and tear and keeping it clean and dry when not in use are recommended practices, along with rinsing the propeller blades and remote control with fresh water after use.

Is there a warranty on the Stoke Foil Boost?

Yes, the Stoke Foil Boost comes with a 12 month warranty covering manufacturing defects. We also offer an additional 12 month limited warranty on parts and labour against any manufacturing defects. Refer to our warranty page for further information.

Battery

Your Stoke Foil Boost relies on a battery to power the electric motor that propels you across the water. The battery stores energy, providing the power needed for exciting boosts and effortless cruising.

The Stoke Foil Boost features a unified battery system, seamlessly integrating the battery, motor, and control unit. This streamlined design maximises efficiency and performance, ensuring every watt of power is put to good use.

The ideal battery for your Stoke Foil Boost depends on your riding style and preferences. Choose the battery that best matches your needs and unlock the full potential of your hydrofoiling experience.

Here are the battery options available for your Stoke Foil Boost:

Sports Battery

Sports Battery - our flagship battery which suits majority of riders. Perfect balance between weight, minimal drag and capacity (apx. 50 x 15s boosts).

XR Battery

The XR Battery is perfect for heavier riders or those using smaller foils and boards that drain the battery faster. Good fit for long downwinders where you have the genuine need for more boosts (apx. 75x15s boosts).

Receiver

Your Stoke Foil Boost relies on a receiver to maintain a strong connection between your handheld controller and the motor. The receiver acts as a communication hub, ensuring precise control and responsiveness throughout your ride.

The Stoke Foil Boost offers two receiver options to suit your board and riding style:

Underboard Receiver

Underboard receiver - turns the whole board into an antenna. The under-board antenna works on the majority of boards on the market, however if you have a board with a carbon mast track we recommend using our over board receiver. Cannot be used as a on-board option.

Overboard Receiver

Overboard receiver - standard receiver which you place on top of the board. Perfect for those who have boards with carbon foil tracks. Does not work when submerged under water.
